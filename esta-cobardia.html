    Overall Meaning

    The lyrics of Frankie Ruiz's song "Esta Cobardia" speaks of a love that is both strong and silent. The singer is in love with a woman who is unaware of his feelings towards her. He keeps his love hidden, and when she is near, he tries his best not to give himself away. The singer's heart leaps every time she comes by, and the mere sight of her sets his heart ablaze. He longs for her so much that he is willing to give up his life just to be with her.


    The singer expresses his frustration and sadness, knowing that his love for her is nothing but a distant dream. He is too afraid to confess his feelings to her, and so his love remains unrequited. He sees her as a star shining so brightly in the sky, too beautiful and too far away for him to reach. He has given her everything he has, even the kisses she never asked for, and yet she remains oblivious to his love.


    Despite his pain, the singer accepts his fate, acknowledging that his fear and cowardice have caused him to lose any chance of winning her heart. The song ends with a melancholic realization that he may never be able to have her, and he will always see her as that beautiful star he can never reach.
