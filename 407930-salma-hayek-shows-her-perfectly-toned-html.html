    Salma Hayek set cyberspace ablaze with some sizzling fresh snaps. The stellar actress shared an image of time spent in a sauna to destress, and her fanbase is utterly smitten.

    In the eye-catching images, Hayek gives a glimpse into her self-care regimen. It involves some intense perspiration in the warmth of a sauna. She shared two snapshots where she's lounging on a wooden sauna bed, modestly draped in two white towels that strategically cover her chest and hips.

    “Embracing the healing power of the sauna and sweating out the stress this #WorldWellbeingWeek ," the "Magic Mike" star aptly captioned the post.

    While some admirers were thrilled by Hayek's impeccable physique, others cast aspersions, insinuating that they too could defy the years if they were married to a billionaire, referencing Hayek's spouse, François-Henri Pinault.

    However, the actress's fans were quick to leap to her defense, noting that her flawless skin is akin to that of Jennifer Lopez. They also pointed out her natural beauty, asserting that even if she had undergone any cosmetic procedures, it's incredibly difficult to tell.

    Salma Hayek hasn't cultivated the discipline to get in early morning workouts. Her bustling schedule leaves little room for regular gym sessions, as she often finds herself engrossed in work, with her days sometimes stretching up to 20 hours.

    Yet, she doesn't completely dismiss the importance of physical activity. Even if she doesn't have the time for intensive workouts, she's learned the art of passive body training. A woman in London has taught her to hold her body in such a way that her muscles are engaged throughout the day, even during mundane tasks like brushing her teeth.

    Salma Hayek's secret weapon for keeping fit is restorative yoga. The actress has learned to tone her muscles without tensing them. She focuses on utilizing the necessary parts, but always without strain.
