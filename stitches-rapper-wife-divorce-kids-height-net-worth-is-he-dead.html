    There are more dramas surrounding this guy, let us learn more about his biography, career, wealth, family and even body traits, we have covered almost everything.

    Stitches’ Biography,

    Everyone knows that Stitches was born to Esther and Alex Katsabanis on June 17, 1995 and his birthplace was in Miami, Florida, United States. He is one of the non-black rappers in the music business, of Cuban and Greek descent. His parents’ union ended when he was one year old and he has two older siblings from his parents’ marriage – brothers Alex Jr and Dimitri. He attended G. Holmes Braddock High School in Miami-Dade County, where he later dropped out.

    Rumor has it that he was expelled from school for punching the principal in the face. According to the story, Stitches moved to South Beach, Florida and got into selling drugs and guns to make ends meet. This report is corroborated by several claims from his siblings; obviously, his bad boy act started long before he hit the music scene and won over hip-hop fans.

    Stitches began his rap career under the stage name Lil Phil. Back in 2012, he released the track, My Name Lil Phil but he ditched the moniker at age 16 and started performing under the stage name Stitches. His debut mixtape titled, No Statement Is My Statement was released in 2014. The tape spawned the hit song, Brick in the Face and its video has since garnered over 26 million views on YouTube. In 2015, he went further to release two more mixtapes, Brick Bible and Soft and Ask and eventually followed up with the release of his debut album, For Drug Dealers Only in the same year.

    The following year, his second album titled Tales of a Drug Lord was released. The album included the track Pain and Success which was well received. In 2017, he embarked on the following projects: I Need Rehab, Cocaine Vacation, and The Trap House.

    The rapper’s music is inspired by artists such as Gucci Mane, Whiz Khalifa and Juicy J who have impressed music audiences with their talents. He also tapped into the artistry of acts such as Trace Cyrus, Blac Youngsta, Compton Menace and Fat Trel.

    Stitches’ profile didn’t help counter the stereotype of crime and violence associated with rappers in the music industry. He was arrested individually. in 2017, he faced drug and firearms charges in Miami, Florida. Police found weed, a gun, ammunition and other incriminating items in his possession. His lawyer was able to find him a good plea in the firearms case.
