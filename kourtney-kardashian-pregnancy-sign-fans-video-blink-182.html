    MUSIC lovers have spotted the link between Kourtney Kardashian's pregnancy announcement and an iconic Blink-182 video.

    Kourtney Kardashian, 44, announced she was pregnant with Blink drummer Travis Barker's baby at his band's LA gig last night.

    She delivered the surprise by writing "Travis I'm pregnant" on a large sign, which she held up in front of the stage.

    The scene looked familiar to the group's fans, who instantly recognized the nod to one of the group's breakout hits.

    In the All The Small Things music video, which was released in 1999, a beautiful fan in a bikini wildly waves a sign with the same message on as the trio rock out.

    One fan wrote: "And Kourtney did the Pregnancy reveal. Just Like during the All Small things video."
