    More than 38,000 Amazon shoppers have given the sweatshirt a five-star rating, so you know it must be good. Made from a cotton blend with thick fleece lining for extra warmth, the pullover comes in nine colors and sizes S through XXL. It has ribbed details around the collar, cuffs, and hemline, and it hits around the hip area on most people. Plus, the sweatshirt is tagless, so you don’t have to worry about anything scratching or irritating your skin. 

    You can, of course, wear the pullover with leggings or joggers around the house and to run errands, but it’s cute enough to dress up, too.
