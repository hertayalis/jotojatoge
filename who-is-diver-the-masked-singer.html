    Some fans of 'The Masked Singer' were already sure about who Diver is before Season 10 premiered and they might not even need more clues.

    The Gist:

    • Diver was unmasked as Tom Sandoval.
    • Fans already seemed to have their answer and solved it on their own.
    • Ken was the only one to guess correctly.

    Every season of The Masked Singer features new celebrities whose identities are concealed underneath an elaborate costume. And you have to expect that Season 10 is pulling out all the stops to make the monumental season even more exciting, with unexpected celebrity reveals. However, some fans already thought they knew who Diver is on The Masked Singer before Season 10 even premiered.

    What can we say — sometimes, the clues are there well before judges Ken Jeong, Jenny McCarthy, Nicole Scherzinger, and Robin Thicke have anything to say about it. So, who is Diver in Season 10 of The Masked Singer? Read on to find out who is under the mask.

    Diver on 'The Masked Singer' — The Clues

    A promo for Diver was shared on TikTok weeks before the season premiere. In it, we see a deep-sea scuba diver in an old-fashioned suit. Thanks to the promo, we have a handful of clues:

    • Diver says they are “usually pretty pumped.”
    • They also claim to be “the most hated creature in the ocean right now.”
    • Cardinal

    Episode 2 Clues:

    • Newspaper
    • “I’ve been everyone’s favorite person to hate and, to be honest, I get it.”
    • “I’ve made mistakes but honestly, I did it for love.”
    • Hourglass
    • Suit of armor (or knight?) with two lions on it
    • Compass with its needle pointing to “MW” in place of “West”
    • The phrases “11 million eyes on Diver!” and “Diver opens another business!” (Diver then said, “The more people talk about my business, the more I want them out of it.”)

    Episode 3 Clues:

    • Always wanted to be famous
    • Has modeled, acted, and sung, and is now on TV
    • Red high heels
    • A club, as in the playing card suit. (Diver said, “I’m well-suited for the club — I know how to get the club going.”)

    Diver on 'The Masked Singer' — The Guesses.

    Spoiler alert! While we don't know for sure who is behind the Diver mask, the correct identity may be spoiled in the guesses below.

    Episode 2 Guesses:

    • Nicole: Adam Levine
    • Ken: Tom Sandoval
    • Jenny: Scott Disick

    Episode 3 Guesses:

    • Nicole: Pete Davidson
    • Ken: Tom Sandoval
    • Robin: Pauly D

    So, who is Diver on 'The Masked Singer'? The answer is...

    Diver is Tom Sandoval from Vanderpump Rules! Before his reveal, he made a touching comment about how under the mask, he was only judged for his performance and not his past.

    Watch The Masked Singer on Wednesdays at 8 p.m. EST on Fox.
