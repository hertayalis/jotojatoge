    Trainers, it looks like Niantic Support has encountered another hiccup, but as always, they’re on the lookout for ways to get better.

    A recent interaction caught our eye when a Tweet reply to Pokémon GO Hub about Shiny Lucario, which we know can be shiny, sparked some confusion. A player shared a screenshot of their conversation with Niantic Support asking if Lucario could be shiny.
